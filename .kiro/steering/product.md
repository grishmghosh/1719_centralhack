# Product Overview

**Swasthio** is a comprehensive healthcare management mobile application built with Expo and React Native. The app empowers users to manage their personal health records, medications, family health, and emergency medical information with a focus on accessibility and ease of use.

## Core Features

### **Home Dashboard**
- **QR Health ID**: Quick health record sharing via QR code scanner
- **Today's Health**: Daily medication reminders and health tasks
- **Health Alerts**: Prescription refills, upcoming checkups, urgent notifications
- **My Conditions**: Overview of ongoing health conditions with status tracking
- **Family Health**: Multi-person health management for caregivers
- **Enhanced Health Intelligence**: AI-powered medical document analysis (SATRONIS)

### **Records Management**
- Medical document storage and organization
- AI-powered document processing and summarization
- Secure health record access

### **Community & Family**
- **Community**: Health-related social features and support networks
- **Family**: Manage health records for multiple family members
- **Emergency Profile**: Critical health information for emergency situations

### **Profile & Settings**
- Standalone profile screen (accessible via header icon)
- Account settings and preferences
- Privacy and security controls

## Target Platforms
- **iOS**: Primary platform with tablet support
- **Android**: Full React Native support
- **Web**: Single-page application for desktop access

## Key Characteristics
- **Healthcare-focused design**: Purpose-built for medical information management
- **Emergency-ready**: Offline access to critical health data
- **Modern UI**: Floating dock navigation, glass morphism effects, smooth animations
- **Minimal aesthetic**: Clean design with Outfit and Inter font families
- **Brand colors**: Teal and green palette (#0D7377, #14A085, #7FCDCD)
- **Accessibility**: High contrast, clear typography, intuitive navigation
- **Cross-platform**: Optimized for iOS, Android, and web
